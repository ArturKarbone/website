Trainer

 Alexey Krivitsky
Certified Scrum Trainer® with the ScrumAlliance

Alexey is Certified ScrumTrainer, the author of the mini-book Agile Retrospective Kickstarter and a hands-on Lean-Agile practitioner with the first Scrum experiment dated back to 2003-2005. Alexey kicked off the AgileUkraine community in 2007, co-produced dozens of Agile events, including the sounding Agile Eastern Europe Conferences.

Alexey has been coaching teams and organizations. Worked as a software developer, ScrumMaster, agile coach and a business trainer. He is a public speaker and an often guest at Agile conferences.

Alexey is also a Certified LeSS (Large Scale Scrum) Practitioner® and a Team Coach with Erickson International.

By attending the trainings of Alexey you’ll be exposed to the wide experience, lots of stories and the latest training techniques, including the one of the most popular simulations with Scrum: www.lego4scrum.com invented by Alexey and translated now to more than 15 languages.
